Types of Website

Explore the diverse world of websites and their unique purposes in the digital landscape

Blog/Personal Website

Share Your Story

For individuals to share experiences, opinions, or specialized knowledge. Often used to showcase a portfolio or establish thought leadership in a particular niche.

Popular Platforms:

  • WordPress
  • Medium
  • Blogger
  • Personal portfolios

E-commerce Website

Sell Online

A site dedicated to selling products or services online. Includes shopping carts, payment gateways, and inventory management systems.

Well-known Examples:

  • Amazon
  • eBay
  • Lazada
  • Shopee

Business/Corporate Website

Professional Presence

Represents a company, its services, and contact information. Its goal is to build credibility, provide information, and generate leads.

Common Features:

  • About Us pages
  • Service listings
  • Contact forms
  • Testimonials

Educational Website

Learn & Grow

Contains educational materials, online courses, tutorials, or reference information. Used by institutions, teachers, and e-learning platforms.

Popular Examples:

  • Khan Academy
  • Coursera
  • web.nlp.gov.ph
  • University portals

Web Portal

Centralized Access

A centralized location that provides access to various resources, news, email, and forums. Common in universities or large organizations.

Common Uses:

  • Intranet portals
  • Student portals
  • Government services
  • Enterprise resource planning

News/Information Website

Stay Informed

Provides news, articles, and information to the public. Ranges from global news organizations to specialized topic sites.

Leading Sources:

  • BBC News
  • CNN
  • The New York Times
  • Reuters

Social Media Website

Connect & Share

Focused on interaction and content sharing between users. Platforms for building communities and sharing ideas.

Major Platforms:

  • Facebook
  • X (Twitter)
  • Instagram
  • LinkedIn

Entertainment Website

Enjoy & Relax

Provides entertainment like videos, music, online games, and memes. Designed for leisure and enjoyment.

Popular Examples:

  • YouTube
  • Netflix
  • Spotify
  • Online gaming platforms

Non-Profit Website

Make a Difference

Showcases an organization's mission, activities, and how people can help or donate. Focuses on cause rather than profit.

Common Features:

  • Mission statements
  • Success stories
  • Donation systems
  • Volunteer sign-up
